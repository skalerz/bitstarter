Is there any wine lover who doesn't dream of a great real cellar filled with hundreds of incredible wines ? But how many of us do really have the opportunity to fulfill such a dream ? Even though electronic cellars make it more accessible, this is often hard to initiate your cellar with wines that can be drunk rightaway, correctly balanced with older and better ones.

What we offer @ My Sommelier is a straightforward and easy way to start your journey. You tell us what you like (just sending a picture of your prefered wines), how much room you have, and how much you usually drink, and we take care of everything. We'll balace the most suitable initial cellar for you, taking into account both your budget & taste. We'll spot the best opportunities today to have great wines tomorrow, and at the same time, we'll grant you an access to ready to drink bottles rightaway.

FAQ

How much do I need to start a cellar ?
At My Sommelier, we believe that everybody should be able to start something interesting, even with small amounts of money. Basically what tends to happen with wines today is that we buy younger wines because we have little money. What we offer is the opportunity for you to replace the fresh and too young wines with older and better ones just at the cost of some organization - that we will bring you. We believe that you can start something very decent with an inital amount of only $400 - that is even cheaper than your smallest electronic cellar...
If this is so simple, why are you the only one to offer this ?
Because wine is both an art (create, assembling, ...) and a science, we often only focus on one part of the subject. You can find people taking care of the art : helping you taste great wines, and of course selling some to you ;). You can sometimes find financial people who invest in wine. What we bring is the best of both worlds : @ My Sommelier, you can find the best Sommeliers out of France, and great Engineers. Together, we offer you state of the art optimizations to bring the cheapest way to invest in the perfect cellar, tailor made for your taste.
